# NebulusDB Vision

NebulusDB exists to empower developers with a modern, flexible, and powerful embedded database that works everywhere—Node.js, browser, desktop, edge, and serverless. Our vision is to make data management seamless, secure, and delightful for every developer, regardless of platform or scale.

---

## Mission
To provide the most developer-friendly, extensible, and portable embedded database for modern applications, enabling rapid innovation and robust data management from prototype to production.

---

## Core Values
- **Developer Experience First:** Simple, intuitive APIs and world-class TypeScript support.
- **Flexibility:** Pluggable adapters and plugins for any environment or use case.
- **Performance:** Fast queries, efficient storage, and smart indexing by default.
- **Portability:** Run the same code in Node.js, browsers, edge, and serverless with no friction.
- **Extensibility:** Easy to extend with custom plugins, adapters, and integrations.
- **Transparency:** Open source, open governance, and a welcoming community.

---

## Long-Term Goals
- **Universal Embedded Database:** Be the go-to choice for local-first, offline-first, and cross-platform apps.
- **Rich Ecosystem:** Foster a vibrant community of plugins, adapters, and tools.
- **Seamless Sync:** Make syncing and replication as easy as local storage.
- **Security by Default:** Provide robust encryption, access control, and audit features.
- **Cloud & Edge Ready:** Integrate natively with serverless and edge platforms, with optional cloud sync.
- **Best-in-Class Tooling:** Offer devtools, browser UIs, and CLI tools for every stage of development.

---

## Why NebulusDB?
- **No server required:** Embedded, serverless, and ready to use anywhere.
- **Modern stack:** Built for TypeScript, ES modules, and the latest JS features.
- **Hackable:** Designed for customization and rapid iteration.
